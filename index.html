Uber Data Extractor

Export your Uber history to CSV via a nifty Chrome extension

How to use:

  1. Install the extension from the Chrome Web Store
  2. Visit https://riders.uber.com/trips and login if you aren't already
  3. Click the new map icon you should have in the top right of your Chrome browser. Note, this may take a few minutes to finish!
  4. Voila, you have a handy CSV of all your Uber history
  5. BONUS STEP: Drop your CSV into the Uber Data Visualizer to aggregate and visualize your Uber usage
