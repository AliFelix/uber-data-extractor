Uber Trip History

Export your Uber history to CSV

How to use:

  1. Drag and drop the icon above to your bookmark bar
  2. Visit https://riders.uber.com/trips and login if you aren't already
  3. Click "Uber Export" from your bookmarks bar and wait for the process to complete
  4. Voila, you have a handy CSV of all your Uber history
  5. BONUS STEP: Drop your CSV into the Uber Data Visualizer to aggregate and visualize your Uber usage
